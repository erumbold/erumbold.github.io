A Critical Analysis of Objective Evaluation Metrics for Music Source Separation Quality

Erika Rumbold | erumbold@u.northwestern.edu | Thesis for M.S. in Computer Science | Advised by Prof. Bryan Pardo

Overview

Here you will find the source separated audio data, and the subjective evaluation data of that audio from my thesis. For more information on this data, please refer to my thesis document.

Mean Opinion Scores

For these experiments, I used a 30-song subset of the MUSDB18 dataset for music source separation. I source separated each song using these five source separation models:

  1. Hybrid Demucs [1]
  2. Demucs-Extra [2]
  3. D3Net [3]
  4. Spleeter [4]
  5. Source Separation Wavenet [5]
The Mean Opinion Scores for this data can be downloaded here. There are two scores for each example, one related to "presence of Artifacts" and one related to "presence of Other Instruments."
