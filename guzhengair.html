What is the GuzhengAir

As the name suggests, the GuzhengAir is an electronic instrument designed to simulate the playstyle and unique sound of a traditional Chinese guzheng. To play the GuzhengAir, place a finger or hand (typically the thumb) in front of the main ultrasonic sensor. Then, when the finger is lifted, a note is played (to resemble plucking a string). The instrument is tuned to the G-pentatonic scale, range D4-B5; and the pitch that iss played is determined by the finger's proximity to the sensor - lower notes are played closer to the sensor.
